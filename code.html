What is entity-based SEO and how is it different from keyword SEO? add
Keyword SEO is about ranking for specific search terms. Entity SEO is about making Google and AI recognise your brand as a trusted expert — so they recommend you even when someone doesn't search your exact name.
How is programmatic SEO different from content SEO? add
Regular content SEO means writing pages one at a time. Programmatic SEO uses templates to create hundreds of pages at once — like a page for every city you serve. It's the same quality, just at a much bigger scale.
How long does SEO take to show results? add
Technical fixes show up in Google within 2–4 weeks. You'll usually start seeing ranking improvements in 3–6 months. After that, your content keeps working for you — bringing visitors month after month without paying for ads.
What does a Digitalbuddha SEO audit include? add
We check your entire website — page speed, how Google finds your pages, your content, your links, and what competitors are doing better. You'll get a clear priority list of what to fix first for the biggest results.
Does Digitalbuddha do SEO for e-commerce businesses? add
Yes. Online stores have unique challenges like duplicate product pages and keeping seasonal content fresh. We've worked with fashion, electronics, and retail brands, including helping them show up on Google Shopping.
Is Digitalbuddha suitable for startups and small businesses? add
Yes, absolutely. We tailor our services to fit your budget and stage of growth. Whether you're just starting out or scaling fast, we'll build an SEO foundation that grows with your business.
